Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our hedge fund and investment services.

What is the minimum investment required?

Our standard minimum investment is $250,000, though we offer special programs for accredited investors starting at $100,000. For institutional clients and family offices, we can discuss customized arrangements based on your specific needs and investment goals.

How are your fees structured?

We operate on a "2 and 20" fee structure common in the hedge fund industry: 2% management fee on assets under management and 20% performance fee above a high-water mark. However, we offer discounted fees for larger investments and longer lock-up periods. All fees are clearly outlined in our investment agreement with no hidden charges.

How often will I receive performance reports?

Clients receive detailed quarterly performance reports with comprehensive analysis of their portfolio. Additionally, we provide monthly statements with key performance metrics. Our online portal offers real-time access to your portfolio's performance, and your relationship manager is always available for consultation.

What makes your hedge fund different from others?

Three key differentiators: 1) Our proprietary quantitative models combined with fundamental analysis create a unique edge. 2) We offer unparalleled transparency with full position-level reporting. 3) Our client service is exceptional with dedicated relationship managers and regular investment committee updates. Plus, our performance track record speaks for itself.

How do you manage risk in volatile markets?

Our multi-layered risk management approach includes: strict position sizing limits, sector diversification, hedging strategies, real-time risk monitoring systems, and regular stress testing of portfolios against historical crisis scenarios. Our risk management team works closely with portfolio managers to ensure all investments align with our risk parameters.
